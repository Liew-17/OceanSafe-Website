How donations are used?

Donate RM 5

Supports the purchase of reusable gloves for volunteers participating in beach cleaning activities.

Donate RM 10

Helps fund educational materials for one student attending a marine conservation workshop.

Donate RM 25

Contributes to the purchase of seedlings for one mangrove tree in a marine restoration project.

Donate RM 50

Assists in the maintenance of a coral nursery, aiding in the restoration of damaged coral reefs.

Donate RM 100

Provides necessary equipment for underwater clean-up dives, ensuring safer and more effective marine debris removal efforts.
