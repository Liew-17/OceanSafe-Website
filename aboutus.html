About Us

Embark on a journey to discover our commitment to ocean conservation, get to know our passionate team members, and meet our visionary founders dedicated to safeguarding marine ecosystems.

Welcome to OceanSafe

Welcome to OceanSafe, where our dedication to ocean conservation knows no bounds. Established in 2020, OceanSafe has emerged as a frontrunner in the realm of marine preservation. Our fervent commitment to safeguarding marine ecosystems and advocating for sustainability serves as the cornerstone of our endeavors. Comprising a dynamic team of environmentalists, scientists, and passionate advocates, we unite under a common mission: to enact meaningful change on a global scale. Our collective efforts are propelled by a shared sense of responsibility towards the planet and a deep-rooted desire to protect its most precious resource—the ocean.

Mission

To safeguard the world's oceans for future generations, we foster a global community dedicated to ocean conservation and sustainability. Through collaborative efforts, we strive to ensure the health and vitality of marine ecosystems, preserving them as vibrant sources of life and inspiration for all.

Vision

We envision a future where the oceans flourish as vibrant ecosystems, supporting life and sparking wonder. Through technology, education, and collaboration, we empower global efforts to ensure their health and vitality. Together, we strive for a world where future generations inherit thriving oceans brimming with life, beauty, and opportunity.

Goal

Our goal is to drive global change in ocean conservation and sustainability. Through strategic partnerships and initiatives, we aim to protect marine biodiversity, promote sustainability, and raise awareness. By fostering collaboration and empowering communities, we envision thriving oceans as resilient ecosystems, providing essential resources for generations to come.

Our History

Our history is a testament to our unwavering commitment to ocean conservation. OceanSafe was founded in 2020 by Liew Yi Qi, driven by a shared vision of protecting marine ecosystems and promoting sustainability worldwide. As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our commitment to protecting our oceans and preserving the beauty and wonder of our planet. Our history serves as a reminder of how far we've come and inspires us to continue our journey towards a more sustainable and resilient world for generations to come.
