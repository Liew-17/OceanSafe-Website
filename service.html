Community Service

We host up to six weekly community cleanup events to protect our oceans. Join us as we work together to keep our beaches and coastal areas clean and safe for marine life.

Community Service: Join Us for a Cleanup Event

Join our community cleanup event and make a difference for our oceans! Together, we'll gather at local beaches or coastal areas to remove trash and debris, preventing pollution and protecting marine life. These events are open to all ages and provide an opportunity to contribute to ocean conservation efforts while connecting with like-minded individuals.

Get Involved: Volunteer and Support Ocean Conservation

Volunteers are the heart of our community service initiatives. Whether you're passionate about protecting marine life, reducing plastic pollution, or restoring coastal habitats, there are many ways to get involved:

  • Join a cleanup event near you and lend a hand in keeping our oceans clean and healthy.

  • Spread awareness about ocean conservation by sharing information on social media and engaging with your local community.

  • Organize your own cleanup event or fundraising campaign to support ocean conservation organizations and initiatives.

  • Advocate for policy changes and sustainable practices that promote the health and preservation of our oceans.

No matter how big or small your contribution, every action counts in safeguarding our oceans for future generations.
